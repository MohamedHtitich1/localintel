Transform Selection ▼

Transform and spline are two separate layers. Transform moves data to unconstrained scale before fitting; predictions are back-transformed after.

log — 7 indicators

Strictly positive values, proportional changes.

Forward: y → log(y)
Back: ŷ → exp(ŷ)
Guarantee: exp(x) > 0

logit — 55 indicators

Proportions (0–100%) with floor/ceiling effects.

Forward: p → log(p/(1-p))
Back: ŷ → 100/(1+e-ŷ)
Guarantee: result ∈ [0, 100]
